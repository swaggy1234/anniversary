# 💕 Anniversary Gift Card 💕

A beautiful, interactive animated gift card for your special someone! This card features a stunning 3D flip animation, romantic design, and interactive gift selection that sends the chosen gift directly to your email.

## ✨ Features

- **🎨 Beautiful Design**: Pinterest-inspired aesthetic with soft pastels and romantic gradients
- **🔄 3D Flip Animation**: Smooth card flip with CSS 3D transforms
- **💕 Floating Hearts**: Animated hearts that float across the screen
- **📸 Photo Gallery**: Space for your special memories
- **🎁 Gift Selection**: 6 different anniversary experience options
- **📧 Email Integration**: Automatically sends gift choice to your email
- **📱 Responsive**: Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile

## 🎯 How It Works

1. **Click the sparkling seal** to open the card
2. **Read the love note** and view photo memories
3. **Select your perfect gift** from 6 romantic options
4. **Click "Send My Choice"** to email your selection
5. **Enjoy your special anniversary!** 💕

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Clone this repository**:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/swaggy1234/anniversary-present.git
   ```

2. **Open the card**:
   - Simply open `anniversary-gift-card.html` in any web browser
   - No server required - it's a pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript file!

3. **Customize for your love**:
   - Edit the love note in the HTML file
   - Add your own photos to the gallery
   - Modify the gift options to match your preferences
   - Update the email address in the JavaScript

## 💌 Customization

### Changing the Love Note
Edit the text inside the `.love-note` div in the HTML file.

### Adding Your Photos
Replace the placeholder photo items in the `.photo-gallery` section with your actual images.

### Modifying Gift Options
Update the gift options in the `.gifts-grid` section to match your preferences.

### Changing the Email
Update the email address in the `submitChoice()` function in the JavaScript section.
